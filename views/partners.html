Partnership & Collaboration

It is prerequisite for either students or freshly graduates to have at least a year or so of internship experience and published journals in order to pursue further studies or even for job hunting. In a world that requires a “job experience” for getting a “job”, one needs an internship or training experience. Luckily, this is provided by our publishing partner groups namely –

IJCRT

The International Journal of Creative Research Thoughts is an UGC approved research journal, peer-reviewed, and referred journals, and open access journals. It provides a platform for students and graduates to publish their works and aims to explore advances in research pertaining to applied, theoretical and experimental technological studies. Their goal is to promote scientific information interchange between researchers, developers, engineers, faculty, students, and practitioners working in and around the world.

IJARESM

The International Journal of All Research Education and Scientific Methods, ISSN: 2455-6211,>5 Impact Factor, SJR: 2.28 is a scholarly online, UGC certified, open access, peer-reviewed, multi-disciplinary,It is an international scientific journal that aims at promoting research in all the research fields like Engineering, Science Technology, Education, Management, Medical Sciences, Dental Sciences, Agriculture Sciences, Social Sciences, Health Care, Arts & Humanities and many more.

IJRAR

The International Journal of Research and Analytical Reviews is one of the leading high quality Scholarly open access and peer reviewed quarterly published research journal. IJRAR is providing a platform for the researchers, academicians, professional, practitioners and students to impart and share knowledge in the form of high quality empirical and theoretical research papers, case studies, literature reviews and book reviews.

IJERSTE

The International Journal of Enhanced Research in Science Technology and Engineering, ISSN: 2319-7463, >5 Impact Factor, UGC approved, was published in the year 2012 in India. It is an international academic journal which invites unpublished novel, original, empirical and high quality research work pertaining to recent developments and practices in the areas of Computer Science & Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication, Electrical and Electronics, Electronics & Telecommunication, Mechanical Engineering, Textile Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Biotechnology, Instrumentation, Automation, Sensors, Control System, Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Civil Engineering, Modeling and Simulation, VLSI, DSP, Image Processing, Neural Network and Fuzzy Logic, Biology, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Bio-Chemistry, Food Technology, Algorithms, and emerging paradigms in allied subjects.
