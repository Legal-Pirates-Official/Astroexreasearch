RESEARCH TRAINING PROGRAM FOR STUDENT/ GRADUATE RESEARCH TRAINEE (SRT/GRT):

  • Opening for two months virtual research training (extension will be also provided based on research work, performance and outputs).
  • The students/scholars pursuing or completed K12, BEngg/BTech/BSc/MS/MSc/MEngg/MTech is open to apply for this programme.
  • Fluent in personal as well as technical skill both for oral and written.
  • Must be open to interact, communicate and act as volunteer for team.
  • Basic knowledge in PowerPoint, Excel and Word.

BENEFITS OF TRAINING PROGRAMME:

  • Guidance and mentorship support from research experts for carrying research in training programme.
  • Certificate of appreciation will be provided at time of completion of training programme.
  • Letter of recommendation from research supervisor as well as community board members.
  • Publication in UGC as well as peer-reviewed based journals with impact factor ≥ 5.
  • Top 5 research trainee will be awarded with certificate of excellence.
  • Top 3 research trainee will be awarded with fellow membership certificate
  • Career guidance for higher studies for research projects/papers
  • Chances to get hired among our core team members

Limited seats available: 30-35
